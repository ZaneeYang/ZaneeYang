My work is driven by a simple question: how do we help people not just find answers, but truly understand how those answers take shape — traceable, verifiable, and grounded in evidence rather than noise.

- 🤖 I am currently an AI Agent Researcher at **Tencent FinTech**, focusing on **AI Agents**, especially **Deep Search** — building agentic systems that autonomously search, connect, and verify scattered evidence across the web, then reason over it to produce grounded answers that people can trust and act on with confidence.
