✨**Let's explore the wonderful world with curiosity!**


- 💡 As information becomes increasingly abundant, I believe the central challenge is no longer access, but understanding. My work is driven by a simple question: how can we help people move beyond finding answers to understanding how those answers are formed — traceably, verifiably, and grounded in evidence rather than noise?

- 🤖 I am currently an AI Agent Researcher at Tencent FinTech, focusing on AI Agents, especially Deep Search. My work explores how to build agentic systems that can autonomously search, connect, and verify scattered evidence across the web, then reason over it to produce grounded answers that people can trust and act on with confidence.

- 🎓 I received my master's degree from the School of Future Technology, South China University of Technology, advised by Prof. Jin Xu. I also worked with the MAGIC Research Group at Xi'an Jiaotong University on multi-view graph clustering. I received my B.Eng. from Hefei University of Technology in 2023, where I was awarded the National Scholarship (<1%) and Anhui Outstanding Graduate (<1%).
