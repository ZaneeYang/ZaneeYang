My work begins with a simple question: as information becomes more abundant, has our capacity for understanding grown with it?

I focus on **Deep Research** — building agentic systems that search, connect, and verify scattered evidence, then reason over it to produce grounded answers. I care not only about finding answers, but about helping people understand how those answers take shape.
